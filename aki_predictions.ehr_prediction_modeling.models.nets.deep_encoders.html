get_regularization_penalty() → Tensor¶

Compute embedding regularization penalty for SNREncoder.

SNR Encoder is composed of three kinds of weight variables - weights of the SparseLookUpEncoder, weights of the sub-networks, and the connections between different sub-networks. This section separates these variables and applies regularisation.

Returns:

Embedding regularization penalty.
